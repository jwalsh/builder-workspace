* Anti-Goals

- *General-purpose platform*: ExitInterviewAnalyzer solves a specific problem, not a platform for all problems in this domain. Building a platform leads to feature sprawl and loss of core value.
- *Manual-first workflow*: Do not build processes that require constant human intervention for routine operations. If a human must babysit it, the automation has failed.
