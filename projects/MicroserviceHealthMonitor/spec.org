* Overview

A comprehensive observability platform for microservices architectures, providing real-time health metrics, dependency mapping, and anomaly detection.

* Foundational Axiom

Healthcare tools fail when they optimize for data collection over clinical workflow; MicroserviceHealthMonitor embeds clinical reasoning into the system's structure.

* Anti-Goals

- *General-purpose platform*: MicroserviceHealthMonitor solves a specific problem, not a platform for all problems in this domain. Building a platform leads to feature sprawl and loss of core value.
- *Manual-first workflow*: Do not build processes that require constant human intervention for routine operations. If a human must babysit it, the automation has failed.
- *Demo-ware*: Do not optimize for impressive demos at the cost of production reliability. A system that works in a demo but fails under real conditions is worse than no system.
